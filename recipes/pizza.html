Recipe for Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

Dough Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (or bread flour for chewier crust)
  • Warm water
  • Yeast (active dry yeast or instant yeast)
  • Sugar (to feed the yeast)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • chicken
  • bell peppers
  • onions

Steps to make Pizza

  1. Activate & mix dough (5 min): In a bowl, stir warm water + sugar + yeast. Wait 5 min until foamy. Add flour, salt, 1 tbsp oil. Mix, then knead 5–7 min until smooth (add a sprinkle of flour if sticky).
  2. Rise (45–60 min): Lightly oil the bowl, put dough in, cover, and let it double in size.
  3. Preheat & prep toppings (10 min): Heat oven to 475°F / 245°C. In a pan, cook chicken with a little olive oil + pinch of salt until done. Lightly soften onions & peppers (1–2 min) in the same pan.
  4. Shape the crust (2–3 min): Oil a baking sheet or pizza pan. Press/stretch dough into a thin round (12–14"). Poke a few holes with a fork to prevent bubbles.
  5. Assemble: Brush dough with olive oil (this is the “sauce”). Add mozzarella, then chicken, peppers, onions. Lightly salt if you like.
  6. Bake (10–14 min): Bake until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly. Rest 2 minutes, slice, enjoy!
